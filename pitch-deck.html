How It Works

Three phases, zero escort overhead

01 — DETECT

Lock on

Vendor walks in. YOLOv8n detects them, ByteTrack assigns a persistent ID. Bot begins following at safe distance.

0.78 confidence · tested on DC floor
02 — FOLLOW

Escort

PID steering keeps the bot centered on target. Ultrasonic sensor prevents collisions. Camera records rack state as it moves.

HC-SR04 sonar · L298N motor driver
03 — REPORT

Log everything

Every frame, distance reading, and rack scan uploads to the dashboard in real time via WebSocket. Full audit trail, no human needed.

FastAPI · MJPEG stream · scan log
