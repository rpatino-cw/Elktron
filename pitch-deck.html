The Problem

Skilled technicians stuck on autopilot tasks

At CoreWeave data centers, DCTs spend hours each shift on repetitive physical work — seating optics, escorting vendors, inspecting racks — instead of higher-value engineering.

The Solution

Two robots. One dashboard. Zero idle techs.

SO-101 Arm

Autonomous optic seating via imitation learning. A tech demonstrates the task, the arm learns the motion, then runs on its own.

LeRobot + ACT Policy

Escort Bot

Person-following vendor escort. Locks onto the vendor, maintains safe distance, frees up the tech for real work.

Pi 5 + OpenCV DNN + gpiozero

Dashboard

Unified control panel — arm status, escort position, camera feeds, scan logs. One screen for the whole robotic crew.

FastAPI + WebSocket
