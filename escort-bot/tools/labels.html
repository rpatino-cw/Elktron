Escort Bot — Label Print Guide

How to Print

  1. Click Print Labels below (or Cmd + P)
  2. Set paper to US Letter (8.5" x 11")
  3. Set margins to None or Minimum
  4. Check "Background graphics" (needed for power labels + branding)
  5. Scale: 100% (do not fit-to-page)
  6. Print to paper or Save as PDF first to preview

How to Cut

  • Cut along the dashed lines around each label
  • Corner marks show exact cut points
  • Use a ruler + X-Acto knife for clean edges, or scissors work fine

How to Attach

  • Wire labels: Cut out the full rectangle. Fold at the red FOLD line. Wrap around the wire so text shows on both sides. Secure with a small piece of clear tape.
  • Board labels: Tape flat onto the board or nearby surface with clear packing tape.
  • Power labels: Tape directly onto the battery pack / power bank. Use wide clear tape to seal the whole label (spill protection).
  • Orientation labels: Tape to the top surface of the chassis.
  • Branding: Tape centered on the top plate of the chassis.
Label maker? If you have a Dymo/Brother, use the wire label text directly on 1/2" tape. For boards, use 3/4" tape. This printed sheet is designed for regular paper + tape.
